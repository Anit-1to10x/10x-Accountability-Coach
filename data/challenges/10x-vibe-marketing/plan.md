# 10x Vibe Marketing - 30-Day Plan

## Overview
Master the art of Vibe Marketing - creating authentic, resonant content that connects with your audience on an emotional level. Learn to build a personal brand that attracts opportunities.

## Week 1: Foundation & Brand Identity

### Day 1: Discover Your Vibe
- [ ] Define your core values and passions
- [ ] Identify what makes your perspective unique
- [ ] Research 5 creators whose vibe resonates with you
- [ ] Write your initial brand statement
- [ ] Create a mood board for your brand aesthetic

### Day 2: Define Your Target Audience
- [ ] Create detailed audience personas
- [ ] Research where your audience hangs out online
- [ ] Identify their pain points and aspirations
- [ ] Map their content consumption habits
- [ ] Define your audience's language and tone

### Day 3: Craft Your Brand Story
- [ ] Write your origin story
- [ ] Identify your transformation narrative
- [ ] Create your mission statement
- [ ] Define your brand promise
- [ ] Draft your elevator pitch

### Day 4: Visual Brand Identity
- [ ] Choose your brand color palette
- [ ] Select typography that matches your vibe
- [ ] Create or choose profile imagery
- [ ] Design consistent visual templates
- [ ] Set up brand guidelines document

### Day 5: Content Pillars Foundation
- [ ] Identify 3-5 core content themes
- [ ] Map content pillars to audience needs
- [ ] Create a content pillar document
- [ ] Brainstorm 10 topic ideas per pillar
- [ ] Prioritize topics by audience interest

### Day 6: Your Unique Voice
- [ ] Define your writing tone and style
- [ ] Create a voice guide with do's and don'ts
- [ ] Write 3 sample posts in your voice
- [ ] Get feedback on your voice consistency
- [ ] Refine and document your voice guidelines

### Day 7: Week 1 Review & Planning
- [ ] Review all Week 1 deliverables
- [ ] Identify gaps in your brand foundation
- [ ] Refine your brand strategy
- [ ] Plan Week 2 content creation schedule
- [ ] Celebrate Week 1 completion

## Week 2: Content Creation Mastery

### Day 8: Content Format Mastery
- [ ] Study different content formats (carousels, reels, threads)
- [ ] Identify which formats suit your style
- [ ] Create one piece in each format
- [ ] Analyze engagement potential per format
- [ ] Choose your primary content formats

### Day 9: Hook Writing Mastery
- [ ] Study 20 viral hooks in your niche
- [ ] Learn the 7 hook frameworks
- [ ] Write 10 hooks for your content
- [ ] A/B test hook variations
- [ ] Build your hook swipe file

### Day 10: Storytelling Frameworks
- [ ] Master the Hero's Journey for content
- [ ] Learn the Problem-Agitation-Solution framework
- [ ] Practice the Before-After-Bridge technique
- [ ] Create story templates for your niche
- [ ] Write 3 story-driven posts

### Day 11: Content Calendar Creation
- [ ] Set up your content calendar tool
- [ ] Plan 2 weeks of content ahead
- [ ] Balance content types and pillars
- [ ] Schedule optimal posting times
- [ ] Create a content batch workflow

### Day 12: Call-to-Action Mastery
- [ ] Study effective CTAs in your niche
- [ ] Create CTA templates for different goals
- [ ] Test soft vs hard CTAs
- [ ] Build a CTA library
- [ ] Integrate CTAs naturally into content

### Day 13: Repurposing Strategy
- [ ] Map content repurposing workflows
- [ ] Turn 1 long-form piece into 5 micro-content
- [ ] Set up cross-platform repurposing
- [ ] Create repurposing templates
- [ ] Build your content multiplication system

### Day 14: Week 2 Review & Content Audit
- [ ] Audit all content created this week
- [ ] Analyze what resonated most
- [ ] Refine your content strategy
- [ ] Plan Week 3 growth tactics
- [ ] Celebrate Week 2 completion
